Shepherd

This is a tool to reclaim your sanity. It will look like Twitter on your homescreen, but if you click it, Some other app will open.

How to set it up?

  • Make sure you have kindle app installed
  • Open this URL on iPhone in Safari
  • Open Share menu
  • Click "Add to homescreen"
  • Hide real twitter app in some folder, put icon created by this in the same place twitter was.
  • Enjoy productivity
