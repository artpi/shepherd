Shepherd

This is a tool to reclaim your sanity. It will look like FB on your homescreen, but if you click it, Some other app will open.

How to set it up?

  • Make sure you have kindle app installed
  • Open this URL on iPhone in Safari
  • Open Share menu
  • Click "Add to homescreen"
  • Hide real facebook app in some folder, put icon created by this in the same place facebook was.
  • Enjoy productivity
